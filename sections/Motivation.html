Motivation

Why I made this token

I have seen many tokens that have been successful with adding new unique feature. Some features like redistribution, auto-liquidity. Other tokens applied those features later but usually first ones win at last. So I have focused on the NEW UNIQUE FEATURE. What will make people buy and hold and make the token and community grow? Based on this question, I have invented MANY new unique features. And I have tried to select the best one among them. During the selection, I have thought of that, How about adding ALL of them in a SINGLE token? Each feature is very new and unique. It will attract many people. Adding all of them in a single token will make the token more attractive. And 'all of them in a single token' itself can be a another unique concept.
